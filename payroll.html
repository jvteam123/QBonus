How To Use Granular Overrides

1. Dynamic Override System To simulate atypical deductions (such as an employee electing higher Pag-IBIG contributions or SSS adjustments), simply highlight any value inside the payslip and enter a custom amount.

2. Visual Locking Badges Each modified item switches from a blue AUTO badge to an orange MANUAL badge.

3. Easy Recovery To return a modified field back to its default formula, click the miniature Padlock icon to its right. To restore the entire screen back to its default state, click "Restore Auto-Defaults" at the top.
